We Scare Because We Care

In the city of Monstropolis, monsters generate energy by scaring human children. The power company, Monsters, Inc., employs “scarers” who enter the human world through closet doors. The top scarer, James P. “Sulley” Sullivan, and his best friend and assistant, Mike Wazowski, are excellent at their jobs, until everything changes when a human toddler, later named Boo, accidentally enters the monster world. Because monsters believe children are toxic, Boo’s presence causes chaos. Sulley and Mike try to return her home while uncovering a sinister plot led by fellow scarer Randall, who is developing a machine to forcibly extract screams from children. As they protect Boo, Sulley and Mike discover that laughter produces even more energy than screams, ultimately transforming the entire industry. The film ends with the company shifting to laughter power, Boo returning home, and Sulley becoming the new CEO of Monsters, Inc., ushering in a friendlier, safer era.
